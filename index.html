Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 19, 2025

Short version: We do NOT collect, upload or store any of your images. All processing happens 100% in your browser. Your files never leave your device.

1. Who We Are

ImageTools4Free (imagetools4free.vercel.app) is a free browser-based image tools website offering compression, resizing, conversion, cropping, rotation and watermarking tools.

2. What We Do NOT Collect

  • We do NOT upload your images to any server
  • We do NOT store your images anywhere
  • We do NOT collect your name, email or personal details
  • We do NOT require any account or signup
  • We do NOT sell data to third parties

3. How Our Tools Work

All image processing happens entirely inside your browser using the HTML5 Canvas API. Images are processed locally on your device and downloaded directly to you. No image data is ever sent to our servers.

4. Google AdSense & Cookies

We use Google AdSense to display ads. Google may use cookies to serve ads based on your browsing history. You can opt out at Google Ads Settings. See Google's Privacy Policy for details.

5. Children's Privacy

Our site is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 19, 2025

Short version: We do NOT collect, upload, or store any of your images. All image processing happens 100% inside your browser on your own device. Your files never leave your computer.

1. Who We Are

ImageTools4Free (imagetools4free.vercel.app) is a free online image tools website. We provide tools to compress, resize, convert, crop, rotate, and watermark images directly in your browser.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

We want to be very clear:

  • We do NOT upload your images to any server
  • We do NOT store your images anywhere
  • We do NOT collect your name, email, or personal details
  • We do NOT require any account or signup
  • We do NOT sell any data to third parties

3. How Our Tools Work

All image processing on ImageTools4Free happens entirely inside your web browser using the HTML5 Canvas API. When you upload an image:

  • The image is processed locally on your device
  • The processed image is downloaded directly to your device
  • No image data is ever sent to our servers
  • Your files remain 100% private and secure
